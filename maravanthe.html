Maravanthe Beach

Maravanthe Beach is a serene gem nestled along Karnataka’s west coast, offering a unique and unforgettable experience with its remarkable geography. The stretch of NH66 that passes through Maravanthe is a sight to behold, with the glistening Arabian Sea on one side and the serene Suparnika River on the other. The beach is ideal for those seeking tranquility, as its relatively untouched sands and calm waves create an idyllic escape from the bustle of city life. A walk along the shoreline, with the sound of waves crashing against the sand and the breeze carrying the scent of saltwater, is both soothing and rejuvenating. The Suparnika River, winding through lush green landscapes before merging into the sea, offers a scenic setting for boating and leisurely exploration. Additionally, Maravanthe is a gateway to nearby attractions, such as the ancient Kollur Mookambika Temple and the Kodachadri Hills, which provide opportunities for spiritual and adventurous pursuits alike.

Malpe Beach

Malpe Beach, on the other hand, is a dynamic and vibrant destination, perfect for those who enjoy a mix of leisure and adventure. Located just 6 kilometers from the cultural town of Udupi, Malpe Beach is known for its golden-white sands, turquoise waters, and bustling activities. Visitors can indulge in a variety of water sports, including parasailing, jet skiing, speed boating, and surfing, making it a hub for thrill-seekers. The beach also caters to relaxation, with plenty of shaded spots under swaying palm trees where one can unwind with a book or enjoy the scenic views. A visit to Malpe Beach is incomplete without taking a ferry ride to St. Mary’s Island, a UNESCO-recognized heritage site famous for its striking hexagonal basalt rock formations formed by ancient volcanic activity. The island’s crystal-clear waters and unspoiled beauty make it an ideal spot for picnics and photography. Malpe Beach also has a rich cultural vibe, with local fishermen heading out to sea in traditional boats and colorful beach shacks serving a variety of fresh seafood, including Udupi’s signature coastal delicacies. Whether you’re watching a vibrant sunset or participating in the Malpe Beach Festival held annually, this destination guarantees a memorable experience.
